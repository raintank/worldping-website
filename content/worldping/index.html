worldPing

global application performance at-a-glance.

worldPing is a plug-in for Grafana that continually tests, stores and alerts on the global performance and availability of your Internet applications so you can pinpoint issues, fix them immediately, and improve your user’s experience.

a global footprint you make your own

Monitor performance from all over the planet, across different networks and carriers with dozens of public worldPing probes. Install our open-source collector to measure the performance from within your network, or from a customers location.

get meaningful alerts

Sit back and relax; we’ll let you know when your applications experience a problem. We validate errors across multiple locations, so we won’t annoy you false positives.

reduce the noise

worldPing provides a clean backbone signal from around the world, and automatically learns what performance is “normal” so that you don’t have to.

RAINDEX, our dynamically computed index of performance from every Probe make your alerts even cleaner and more actionable.

pinpoint the problem quickly

Is the problem a regional network issue? DNS, yet again? A slowdown in the backend? worldPing allows you to pinpoint the problem through gorgeous visualizations, giving you context across different layers of the stack.

beautiful Grafana visualizations

Built on Grafana, worldPing is much more than just vanilla network performance monitoring. It uses the power of Grafana to bring together all your data sources and visualize their performance in one gorgeous interface.

ridiculously easy

There’s no software or agent to install or configure. Be up and running with worldPing in less than 60 seconds. Just give us the domain name of your site or application, and we’ll automatically detect what to monitor.

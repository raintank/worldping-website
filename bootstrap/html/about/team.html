matt toback

vp, customer experience

Matt started his career as CFO of a global managed hosting provider, Voxel dot Net. From inception in 1999, he managed the financial operations through years of double-digit growth. Following a successful capital raise, he focused on product design, development and revenue growth as CEO of Ubersmith, a wholly owned subsidiary. As a result of a successful exit to Internap (NASDAQ: INAP), he created a new team of designers and developers to refresh the Internap customer facing tools & creative collateral.

trent white

vp, marketing and design

Trent began in higher-ed as a web developer, art director and adjunct faculty of design.
